Top 3 Movies of 2025

A story about Alan Turing and how he built a machine to break German encryptions during the war. It also handles on the topic of illegal homosexuality and the tolls it took on even the most knoble of people during that time.

A Korean film about a poor family catching a lucky break that turns violent due to their greed.
